# 021: Peer Comparison & Benchmarking

**Status:** Draft
**Epic:** Analytics & Reporting
**Priority:** P3
**Assigned To:** TBD
**Target Release:** v1.3

## 1. Overview

Anonymous peer benchmarking showing how student performance compares to similar students (same condition, age, difficulty level). Helps therapists identify outliers and adjust strategies.

## 2. Key Features

- Anonymous aggregated comparison
- Filter by: condition, age, grade level
- Metrics: completion rate, adaptation success, engagement
- Privacy-first: no identifiable information
- Opt-in for parents/students
- National/regional benchmarks

## 3. Comparison Metrics

- Task completion rate vs peers
- Average time per task
- Adaptation effectiveness
- Progress velocity (improvement rate)
- Engagement score

## 4. Privacy & Ethics

- Full anonymization (no student names)
- GDPR/CCPA compliant
- Opt-in consent required
- Data aggregation minimum 10 students
- No competitive rankings
